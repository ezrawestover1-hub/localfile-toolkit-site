Privacy and files

Converter pages are designed to process supported files locally in your browser. Support does not need your private source files for most issues.

Do not submit card numbers, passwords, government IDs, health information, confidential business files, or other unnecessary sensitive data through support forms or email.

Common questions

These answers cover the most common support paths before you send a request.

Where do I find my Paddle transaction or receipt ID?

Check the official Paddle receipt email sent after purchase. Include that ID when asking about access, billing, or refunds.

Can support recover a file I converted?

No. Converter files are intended to stay in your browser. Keep your own source-file backups and exported copies.

What should I do if purchased access does not appear?

Sign in with the purchase email and try the account restore flow. If access still does not appear, send the purchase email, product name, and Paddle transaction ID through the contact form.

Does a refund request guarantee approval?

No. Refund requests are reviewed under the refund policy, applicable law, and Paddle's merchant-of-record process.
