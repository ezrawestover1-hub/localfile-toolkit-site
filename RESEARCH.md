# Product-line research notes

This lineup was selected to balance one broad search category (image conversion) with four high-intent migration/export problems that can be handled locally in a browser.

- LedgerLift: CSV to IIF for basic QuickBooks Desktop bank transactions.
- PixelPort: common raster image conversion and resizing.
- ContactCraft: vCard/VCF contact exports and CSV spreadsheets.
- CalendarFlow: iCalendar/ICS event exports and CSV spreadsheets.
- CaptionShift: common web/video subtitle text formats.

The selection is based on format prevalence, recurring portability problems, privacy sensitivity, and feasibility without a paid backend. It is not based on a verified paid-keyword-volume dataset. Exact demand and competition should be validated in Google Keyword Planner or an SEO platform before buying domains.

Primary specifications used for implementation direction:
- RFC 5545: iCalendar
- RFC 6350: vCard
- W3C WebVTT specification
- Intuit IIF overview and sample headers

These sites are prototypes. Test with diverse real-world exports before public launch.
